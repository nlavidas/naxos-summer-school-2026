Program Highlights

  • Intensive language classes in ancient and medieval languages
  • Linguistic classes on Historical & Diachronic Linguistics

Funded Research Project

This summer school is part of the research project: "A Corpus-based Valency Lexicon for a Contrastive and Diachronic Study of Ancient & Medieval Languages"

Funded by the Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (HFRI) (No. 20577) within the 3rd Call for HFRI Research Projects to Support Faculty Members and Researchers.
